About Us

Crowsfiddled Company Limited is a Nigerian-based civil engineering and real estate company founded in 2015. With a highly trained team and a commitment to reliability, we provide construction, inspection, and project management services across multiple states.

Our presence has expanded across Jos, Enugu, Anambra, Asaba, Owerri, and Abia State — delivering results that meet engineering standards and exceed client expectations. From soil testing to structural design, we bring expertise and passion to every project.
