About Us

Crowsfiddled Company Limited is a Nigerian-based civil engineering and real estate company founded in 2015.

We operate in Jos, Abuja, Enugu, Anambra, Asaba, Owerri, Lagos, and Abia delivering exceptional engineering results.

Our Services

Crowsfiddled Company delivers top-quality solutions across engineering, construction, and real estate industries.

Civil Engineering

Site inspection, soil testing, load calculations, and structural design led by certified engineers using top-tier tools.

Real Estate Development

End-to-end real estate services — property development, management, and investor partnerships tailored for long-term success.

General Supply & Contracting

We handle procurement and supply of goods and equipment for public and private sector projects across Nigeria.
